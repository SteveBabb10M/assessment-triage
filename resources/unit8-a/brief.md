# Unit 8: Recruitment and Selection — Assignment A

## Task
Investigate how a large business recruits and selects employees, explaining the processes used and the legal and ethical considerations involved.

## Criteria

### P1 — Explain how a large business recruits and selects, giving reasons for their processes
- Explain at least TWO internal and TWO external recruitment methods
- Explain the selection process (e.g. STAR method, bar raiser, assessment centres)
- Give REASONS why the business uses each method
- Cover BOTH recruitment AND selection as separate processes

### P2 — Explain how and why a business adheres to recruitment processes which are ethical and comply with current employment law
- Cover the Equality Act 2010 and its application to recruitment
- Cover at least one other relevant law (e.g. Data Protection Act 2018)
- Explain ETHICAL recruitment practice — fair hiring, non-discrimination, equal opportunities
- Do NOT confuse general business ethics (sustainability) with recruitment-specific ethics

### M1 — Analyse the different recruitment methods used in a selected business
- Go BEYOND listing advantages and disadvantages
- Analyse WHY each advantage matters specifically for the business at its scale
- Use PEEL framework: Point, Evidence, Explain, Link

### D1 — Evaluate the recruitment processes and how they contribute to business success
- Make an OVERALL JUDGEMENT about effectiveness
- Present BOTH sides — strengths AND weaknesses
- Support with evidence from the whole assignment
- Reach a clear CONCLUSION linking recruitment to business outcomes
