# Unit 8 Assignment A — WAGOLL (What A Good One Looks Like)

## Annotated Merit-Level Example Extract

### Internal Recruitment — Job Advert (PEEL structure)

**[POINT]** One internal recruitment method Amazon uses is internal job adverts, where existing employees are notified of vacancies before they are advertised externally.

**[EVIDENCE]** Amazon operates an internal careers portal called "Amazon Jobs" where current employees can view and apply for roles across the company. In 2023, Amazon reported that approximately 30% of their management positions were filled through internal promotion.

**[EXPLAIN]** This method is particularly effective for Amazon because of their enormous workforce of over 1.5 million employees globally. By recruiting internally, Amazon reduces training costs because existing employees already understand the company culture, systems, and processes. This is especially important for Amazon where warehouse operations require specific knowledge of the fulfilment system. Internal recruitment also improves employee retention because staff can see a clear career progression path — if workers believe they can be promoted, they are less likely to leave for competitors. For a company of Amazon's scale, even a small improvement in retention saves millions in recruitment and training costs.

**[LINK]** Therefore, internal job adverts contribute to Amazon's operational efficiency by filling roles faster with employees who require less onboarding, while also supporting their workforce retention strategy.

---

### Why this works at Merit level:
- The POINT is clear and specific
- The EVIDENCE is from the actual business, not generic
- The EXPLAIN goes beyond "it's cheaper" — it explains the MECHANISM (why it's cheaper specifically for Amazon, what the knock-on effects are)
- The LINK connects back to the business's overall effectiveness

- Make a judgement: "On balance, internal recruitment is Amazon's most cost-effective method because..."
- Compare with external methods: "While agencies provide specialist candidates, the internal route is more sustainable for Amazon's high-volume roles because..."
